Juan Cornejo Hernandez

Juan Cornejo Hernandez is a highly accomplished professional with extensive experience in the field of information and communications technology engineering. He obtained his bachelor's degree in informatics from the prestigious Instituto Tecnológico Latinoamericano (ITLA) and later pursued a master's degree in information technology from the Universidad Interamericana para el Desarrollo (UNID). Throughout his career, Juan has demonstrated exceptional leadership skills and a strong commitment to excellence. He honed his leadership abilities by completing a specialty in leadership and institutional management, which has helped him excel in his various roles. Juan began his career as the head of technical support department at the Universidad Tecnológica de Tula-Tepeji (UTTT). In this role, he was responsible for overseeing the coordination of maintenance to computer and telecommunication hardware, as well as integrating and implementing new networks to meet the institution's requirements. Due to his hard work and dedication, he was promoted to the head of planning and evaluation department, where he coordinated and integrated projects aimed at increasing the institutional budget. Juan later joined the Instituto Tecnológico Superior del Occidente del Estado de Hidalgo (ITSOEH) as the planning deputy director, where he was responsible for the allocation and management of the institution's budget. He was later promoted to the management services deputy director, where he managed the budget coming from the federal and state governments. Currently, Juan is the head of the department of information and communications technology engineering at ITSOEH, where he has made significant contributions to the success of the program. Under his leadership, the engineering program has become increasingly popular in the local context, and its graduates have been successfully placed in international tech companies. Juan is a true asset to the field of information and communications technology engineering, and his leadership and expertise are highly valued by his colleagues and peer
